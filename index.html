Top 4 Destinations

Choose among 40+ destinations around the world and 7 different languages - English, French, German, Spanish, Chinese, Japanese, and Italian. Here are top 4 picks from our students this year!

1
New York

    EF New York International Language Center

    New York is America’s capital of art, fashion, culture, finance, and food. Walk around the never-sleeping streets of Manhattan, view the breath-taking skyline from the Empire State Building or enjoy an afternoon picnic in Central Park.

    EF New York’s spacious 25-acre campus spans landscaped gardens and wooded grounds, and offers a traditional campus experience.

    EF New York’s facilities include modern classrooms, newly renovated lounges, Olympic size indoor swimming pool, fitness center, and dance studio.

    Enjoy our campus surroundings with only a 10-minute walk to local shops, cafés and restaurants and excellent transportation connections to explore New York City.

    2
    San Diego

      EF San Diego International Language Center

      San Diego offers a true California experience with beautiful beaches, year-round sunshine and plenty of attractions.

      EF San Diego’s brand new campus school is a 10-story building offering stunning views of the Pacific Ocean.

      Enjoy state-of-the-art facilities including fully equipped gym, dance studio, sand volleyball court, football field, movie theater, cafeteria, café, foosball table and outdoor terrace.

      Learn how to surf, try the city’s mouth-watering fish taco or take a weekend trip to the Grand Canyon.

      3
      London

        EF London International Language Center

        London is vibrantly multicultural yet distinctively British, richly historic but consistently cutting-edge.

        Spin on the London Eye for a bird’s eye view of the city, visit the landmark Big Ben clock tower, or shop around Oxford Street.

        EF London campus is a historic Victorian building that features renovated classrooms, cafeteria, and stylish student lounges, located at the heart of London.

        Our spacious school is walking distance from the London Eye and street-level attractions at Piccadilly Circus and Covent Garden.

        4
        Sydney

          EF Sydney International Language Center

          Golden beaches, beautiful national parks, and sunny climate, Sydney is a diverse, cosmopolitan city offering many outdoor options – from surf lessons and dolphin watching to cinema under the stars.

          Located few steps from Central Park and the central station, EF Sydney is a brand new school boasting bright classrooms with latest teaching technology.

          Enjoy spacious student lounge with pool table, foosball table and video games.

          See the floating opera at the Opera House or get a panoramic view of the Blue Mountains on a weekend excursion.

            A New Way of Learning

            EF’s innovative learning method is based on rapid progress through personalized study. You’ll advance one language level every six weeks – thanks to our combination of classroom lessons, interactive tools and cultural immersion.

            1 – Task based:

            You will practice speaking in real-world situations as often as possible in a wide range of situation and on various subjects.

            2 – Blended learning:

            Advance your language learning faster with our blended learning methodology that combines our Efekta books and technology-enhanced iPad.

            3 – We focus on different learning styles:

            People learn in different ways through seeing (Visual), hearing (Auditory) or creating (Kinesthetic). By mixing these styles, our aim is to make you become quickly independent in your language practice.
